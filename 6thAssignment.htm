Ingredients :

Ingredients

  • Beef minced ½ kg
  • Peas 2 cups
  • Fresh coriander 1 bunch
  • Onions 2
  • Ginger, garlic paste 1 tbsp
  • Salt as required
  • Red chili powder 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder ½ tsp
  • All spices masala mix 1 tsp
  • Tomato (grind) 2
  • Pickle 2 tbsp
  • Green chilies 3
  • Oil ½ cup
  • 1 cup

Method :

  1. In sauce pan put in 🍳
    • Oil
    • Onion
    • Mince ginger garlic paste
    • Salt
    • Red chili powder
    • Termeric powder whole spices powder
    • Tomatoes 🍅
  2. Cook
  3. Well mix mince 🥄
  4. Add peas and tender
  5. Cover and cook till peas tender
  6. Finally add ➕
    • Fresh coriander
    • Pickleand green chilies 🌶
  7. Dish out and serve 🥗
